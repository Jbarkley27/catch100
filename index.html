Catch 100.

The objective of this game is to get to number 100 in the least moves as possible. The player may choose to either add 1, or roll two dice, and add the amount represented by the sum of the dice.

If after a move your position number ends in 5 (5, 15, 25, etc.), an extra 5 is added to your initial position.
